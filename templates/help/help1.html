Dairy Farm management system

Customer signup

By clicking on the signup button, it takes you to the sign up page. For you to sign up into the Dairy Farm management system , You are required to enter the required details on the user sign up page. Then submit. A one time password is sent to the email provided and you can change to a more memorable and favorite password by clicking the 'change password' button. If you have an account already,just log into the system.

Customer Login

By clicking on the login button, it takes you to the login page. For you to log into the system,You are required to provide the username and password provided during signup. After providing the username and the password, you will be logged into the system. Incase you forgot the password,you can click the reset password link that will direct you to the email for the new password.

Once logged into the system, you can buy cows and order milk.
